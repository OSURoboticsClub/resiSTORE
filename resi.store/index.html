What We Do:

The ResiSTORE is managed by members of the OSU Robotics Club. It offers many of the components you need to complete your engineering coursework, design work, and latest personal projects.

We are located in the basement of Dearborn Hall, next to the elevator and staircase.

What We Have:

  • Arduinos
  • Passive Components
  • Discrete Transistors
  • Logic ICs
  • Microcontrollers
  • Amplifier ICs
  • LEDs
  • Motors
  • Connectors
  • Teensy
  • Dev Boards
  • Breakout Boards
  • Motor Controllers
  • Fuses
  • Switches
